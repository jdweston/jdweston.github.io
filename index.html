ETL Pipeline for the 10 Largest Banks by Market Cap

This project presents an end-to-end ETL pipeline that scrapes and processes financial data on the world's 10 largest banks by market capitalization. The final dataset was exported to both a CSV file and a SQLite database using Pandas and SQLite.
